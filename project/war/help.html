Following or about to Follow If an editor is "following" a judge, the judge gets an increase in funding power. If an editor is "about to follow" a judge, she will start following the judge when the next edition becomes current. Until then, funding votes cast by the judge will not benefit from the editor's authority.
Overseen This shows funding activity by the judges the editor is following, during an edition.
Follow and Unfollow If an editor follows a judge, the judge's funding power will increase when the next edition is published. An unfollow will result in a decrease.
Via N Editors This shows the number of editors following the judge when the award was given.
Recently This shows recent activity by the person. If the person is an editor, recent follows and unfollows are displayed. Otherwise, recent funding activity is displayed.
