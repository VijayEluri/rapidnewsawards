Following or about to Follow If an editor is "following" a judge, the judge gets an increase in funding power. If an editor is "about to follow" a judge, she will start following the judge when the next edition becomes current. Until then, funding votes cast by the judge will not benefit from the editor's authority.
Overseen By Editor This shows funding activity by the judges the editor is following, during an edition.
Award Overseen By This shows the editors who were following the judge when the award was given.
Editors Overseeing the Most An editor "oversees" funding if a judge awards money while the editor is following the judge. This page shows how much funding was overseen by each editor during an edition.
Publishing an Edition When newskraft.org publishes the next edition, it becomes the current edition, and all its awards, follows, and unfollows go into effect. Until then, judges can cancel their awards, and editors can cancel their follows and unfollows.
Follows and Unfollows If an editor follows a judge, the judge's funding power will increase when the next edition is published. An unfollow will result in a decrease.
